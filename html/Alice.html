Dont Forget Us Alice...

American Mcgee's Alice is a third person action adventure game, traversing through this twisted version of the Alice in Wonderland story that we all know and love. This game was originally made for PC and Mac OS in 2000, and with very little access to playing this game anymore, sadly. This game got remastered once in 2011, and the only current way legally to access this remaster if you bought the physical version of the sequel, Alice Madness Returns for the Xbox 360 or Playstation 3. This game has become "abandonware", in which a game that is left in the dust and to be forgotten.

This game has been revered in the gaming community for the story aspect of Alice, which is alice struggling with mental health issues. She loses her family in a fire, and post-tragedy deals with survivor guilt and PTSD. She later gets sent to a mental health asylum for dementia, insanity and catatonia. Within Alice's Wonderland, different parts of her real life is reflected into this dark twisted reality.
